# Aurora App - Product Specification

## Vision

**Aurora App is the world's first non-toxic social network and AI counselor for women.**

We help women navigate relationships, conflicts, and life decisions with wisdom, empathy, and community support.

## Core Value Proposition

```
Traditional therapy: $150-300/session, stigma, waitlists
AI companions: Generic chat, no safety context
Safety apps: Physical safety only, no relationship guidance
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Aurora App: Relationship wisdom + Safety philosophy = UNIQUE
```

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Women aged 18-45 who:
- Face relationship conflicts and want unbiased perspective
- Value safety and community
- Seek personal growth and opportunities

**Secondary:**
- University students (campus safety)
- Working professionals (workplace navigation)
- International women (travel safety)

## Aurora Counselor Philosophy

### Core Principles

1. **Empathy First** - Validate feelings before analyzing
2. **Both Perspectives** - Present both sides fairly
3. **No Blame** - Focus on understanding, not judgment
4. **Safety Aware** - Flag concerning patterns (abuse, manipulation)
5. **Actionable** - End with concrete suggestions

### Example Interaction

```
User: "My boyfriend checked my phone without permission. 
       He says he was just worried about me."

Aurora: "I hear how unsettling that feels. Let me share 
        both perspectives:

        His view: He may have genuine worry, especially 
        if something triggered concern. Some partners 
        express care through protective instincts.

        Your view: Privacy is a fundamental need. 
        Checking without permission violates trust, 
        regardless of intention.

        What concerns me: This pattern can escalate. 
        Healthy relationships involve communication 
        before action.

        Suggestion: Have a calm conversation about 
        boundaries. If this becomes a pattern, it's 
        worth deeper reflection.

        How are you feeling about this?"
```

4. **Fast** - LCP < 2.5 seconds
